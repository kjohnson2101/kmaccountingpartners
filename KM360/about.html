About KM360 Media

Business Intelligence for the Leaders Shaping What's Next

KM360 Media is an independent business publication covering Finance and Technology leadership. We exist to inform, recognize, and connect the executives driving meaningful change.

Our Mission

KM360 Media was founded on a simple belief: the best business intelligence comes from people who have actually done the work. Too many publications cover Finance and Technology from the outside. We cover it from the inside.

Our editorial team brings 15+ years of hands-on experience in corporate accounting, ERP administration, AI implementation, and executive recruiting. That background shapes everything we publish — we ask the questions practitioners actually care about and cut through the noise that doesn't matter.

What Makes Us Different

We don't chase page views with clickbait. We don't publish press releases dressed up as analysis. Every article, interview, and award we publish is designed to make Finance and Technology leaders better at their jobs.

Our Leader Spotlight series gives executives a platform to share hard-won lessons. Our Pinnacle Awards recognize leaders who deliver real results — not just titles. Our weekly newsletter distills what matters most so busy executives don't have to read everything to stay informed.
