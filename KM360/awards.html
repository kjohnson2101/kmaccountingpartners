The KM360 Pinnacle Awards

Recognizing Excellence in Finance & Technology Leadership

Annual recognition program honoring the CFOs, CTOs, and senior leaders driving measurable impact. Nominations are open for the 2026 cycle.

2026 Award Categories

🏆
CFO of the Year
Recognizing a Chief Financial Officer who delivered transformational financial leadership, measurable results, and strategic vision in 2026.
💡
AI Innovation in Finance
Honoring the finance or technology leader whose AI implementation created demonstrable business value and operational improvement.
⚙️
Operational Excellence Award
Recognizing outstanding achievement in business process optimization, automation, and operational efficiency across Finance or IT.
🌟
Rising Executive of the Year
Spotlight on an emerging Finance or Technology leader under 40 who is making a significant impact in their organization and industry.
🖥️
Best Technology Implementation
Honoring the most successful ERP, software, or technology platform implementation of the year — on time, on budget, and delivering results.
📈
Revenue Growth Leader
Recognizing a Finance executive whose strategic financial leadership directly contributed to measurable revenue growth in their organization.

Submit a Nomination

Nominations are free and open to all Finance and Technology leaders across industries. You may nominate yourself or someone you know.

Winners are selected by the KM360 editorial panel and announced in November 2026. All nominees receive recognition on the KM360 Media platform.
